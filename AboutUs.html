About Us

My name is Franco Cima and I am a Sophmore at SS. John Neumann and Maria Goretti High School. I found out about the Tech It Out program from my school counselor. She sent an email explaining the program and provided us with the application to apply. The minute I heard it was a program on coding I was immediately interested because coding was a skill I have always wanted to learn. I applied along with a few of my friends and we all got in. Throughout this program I have been given the opportunity to work with and learn from experts in coding. Creating my own website on climate Change has been one of the most game changing experiences of my life. For the first time in my life I have an idea about what I want to make a career out of. I plan on continuing in this field by taking a College Course on Computer Science for my Junior year of high school.
