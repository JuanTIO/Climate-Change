Website Introduction


Welcome to our page on Climate change. This page aims to educate and spread awareness about climate change. This page was created by three students and three mentors for the Pennslyvania UPenn Tech-It Out Program. This page contains information about the causes, effects , and solutions of climate change with a page where you can explore different organiztions and websites to learn and do more. As well as an about me page for the three students who created this page. Enjoy our page and support Climate change action.
